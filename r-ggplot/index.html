R & ggplot2

R and ggplot2 are widely used statistical analysis anddata visalization tools. ggplot2 allows complex visualizations to be constructed by combining simple, consistent, components, such as scales, aesthetics, and geometric marks. This chart shows the relationship between flipper length and body mass for different penguin species with color and size encoding multiple measurements. Unlike the other visalizations, this visualization is static and does not have interactivity.
